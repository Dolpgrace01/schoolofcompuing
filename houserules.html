STATUTES & REGULATIONS

01

Eligibility & Registration

Only verified students of the department with a valid matriculation number are eligible to compete. All athletes must be registered in the official Sports Center database before matchday.

02

Punctuality & Attendance

Teams must arrive at the venue at least 30 minutes before kickoff. A 15-minute grace period is allowed, after which a walk-over will be declared in favor of the present team.

03

Fair Play & Decorum

Unparliamentary language, physical violence, or intimidation of match officials will result in immediate disqualification and possible suspension from all Equity Crew 26 sporting events.

04

Official Kits & Equipment

All players must wear their approved departmental jerseys. Shin guards are mandatory for football matches for player safety. No player will be allowed on the pitch without proper gear.

05

E-Sports (FIFA 26) Guidelines

Standard tournament settings apply: 6-minute halves, Tactical Defending, and World Class difficulty. Any form of 'time-wasting' (passing in the back for more than 5 in-game minutes) is prohibited.

06

Protests & Appeals

Any grievance regarding match results or officiating must be submitted in writing to the Sports Director within 2 hours of match completion.
